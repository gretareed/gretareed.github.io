How to make hot chocolate


You will need the following ingredients:

  • Milk (2cps)
  • Cocoa powder (2 tbsp)
  • Sugar (1/4c)
  • Chocolate chips (1c)
  • Vanilla (1 tsp)
  • marshmallows (optional)


      1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, bring milk to simmer.
      2. Whisk in sugar and cocoa powder and stir until no lumps remain.
      3. Stir in chocolate chips and vanilla and cook, stirring occasionally, until chocolate is completely melted
      4. Pour hot chocolate into mugs, then top with marshmallows or whipped cream.
